Koshary Recipe


Description

Koshary is Egypt's national dish. It is cheap, filling, and delicious. It is made of cooking and mixing together white rice, brown lentils, and pasta, and then topping off with deep-fried onions, salsa, and hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ditalini
  • White long-grain rice
  • Brown lentils
  • Brown onions
  • Salsa
  • Hot sauce
  • Chickpeas

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta, rice, and brown lentils, then put them aside.
  2. Cut brown onions into thin slices and deep fry.
  3. Warm up some salsa and some hot sauce.
  4. Mix your pasta, rice and brown lentils together in a serving plate.
  5. Add some fried onions, salsa, hot sauce, and chickpeas.
  6. Enjoy
