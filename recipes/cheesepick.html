Cheese and Pickle Sandwich Recipe


Description

Grab yourself a cheese and pickle sandwich, one of Britain's favourite sandwiches! Made of your favourite hard cheese, Branston's pickle, and nothing else. Today we'll use mature cheddar.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Mature cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Branstons pickle

Steps

  1. Toast two bits of white bread.
  2. Put butter on the two bits of bread.
  3. Put some cheese between them.
  4. Put some Branston's pickle between them. Don't be shy, slather it on!
  5. Enjoy
